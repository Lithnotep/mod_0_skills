Class: Grill

Attributes:

current_food (array)
is_on (boolean true/false)
number_of_cooks (integer)
temperature (float)

Methods:

add_food() (adds specified food to current_food attribute)
ignition (changes is_on to true)
add_cook() (adds specified amount to number_of_cooks attribute)
increase_heat() (increases temperature attribute by specified amount)
